JAI HARISH

The life of "perippa"

Jai Harish the common man was born to respectable rich family but suffered from diabetes and hence gained so much weight that he requires a crane to lift him up. He is fondly called as peippa (ie) uncle by his beloved friends.His friends took immense care of him as he is an immature baby.
